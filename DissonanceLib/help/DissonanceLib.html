DissonanceLib Compositional application of dissonance curves for SuperCollider


A library of psychoacoustic dissonance curves together with harmonic analysis functions. 

See specific helpfiles for more detail:


 * Dissonance

 * PitchSet

 * LoudnessModel

 * HarmonicVector

 * IntervalTable

 * HarmonicMetric

 * Salience


 additions of psychoacoustic and harmonic math to numbers and collections:

 * numberExtras 

 * liveDissonance    a topic showing how to make dissonance curves out of live sound input with PV Ugens. 



DissonanceLib depends on two other quarks: MathLib and VectorSpace. Optionally, the GNUPlot quark (and GNUPlot itself!) need to be installed in order to plot harmonic spaces and fields in PitchSet.
